Pagandaran Beach


Pangandaran Beach is a tourist destination in Pangandaran Regency, located in the southeast of West Java. The exact location is in Pangandaran Village, Pangandaran District, Pangandaran Regency, West Java Province. Around the tourist sites, visitors can see forest hills, which are nature reserve areas. This forest is a forest where many monkeys, deer, buffalo and many other exotic animals live. Not only that, there are also a series of natural caves and man-made caves built by the Japanese during the Second World War as shelters.
