Panyaweuyan


Panyaweuyan in Majalengka is a hill equipped with terraced valleys with panoramic views of alluring natural beauty. In fact, this place is agricultural land and plantations owned by local residents as in general. However, because this isolated landscape is so amazing that it is often used as a place to take pictures. And now Panyaweuyan Majalengka has revolutionized to become one of the most popular tourist attractions in Majalengka, the latest in this city of wind. The view that is presented is proven to be able to hypnotize many pairs of eyes with all the beautiful views it has. The wide green nature coupled with the cold air because it is at an altitude makes the atmosphere in the Panyaweuyan Valley make you feel at home.
